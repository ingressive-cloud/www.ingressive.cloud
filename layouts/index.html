WireGuard SDN

End-to-End Encrypted Traffic

Traffic between the Ingressive edge and your origin never travels over the public internet unencrypted. A WireGuard-based SDN forms an always-on private tunnel — your origin requires no exposed inbound ports.

  • WireGuard encrypted data plane
  • No open inbound ports on your origin
  • Private connectivity to any cloud or on-prem host
